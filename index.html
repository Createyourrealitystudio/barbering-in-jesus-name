Our Mission

This shop was put on his heart by God, and by faith it was built. We exist to serve God and serve people — creating a wholesome space where families feel welcome, where respect is the standard, and where every single cut, big or small, gets the time and care it deserves.

"Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart,
as working for the Lord, not for human masters." Colossians 3:23

Meet the Barber

Steve Rodriguez

With over 25 years behind the chair, Steve Rodriguez isn't just a barber — he's a man on a mission. A devoted Christian and family man, Steve felt God calling him to create something different: a barbershop that honors the Lord in everything it does.

At Barbering in Jesus' Name, you won't hear foul language or dirty music. You'll walk into a clean, wholesome environment where families — dads, sons, grandfathers — can sit down and feel at home. Steve plays clean music, keeps the conversation uplifting, and treats every person who walks through the door like family.

What started as a humble beginning, built on faith and obedience, is growing into something truly special. Steve shows up every single day, brings his best, and stays grounded in his relationship with God. That's the foundation everything here is built on.
